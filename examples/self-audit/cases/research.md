# Task

Write a decision memo of at most 220 words for a product leader asking whether the company may publicly claim: “AI coding assistants reduce software defects by 30%.” Do not browse. Use only the supplied evidence and state the strongest defensible conclusion, the decision, and the next evidence worth collecting.

Evidence:

- A vendor case series reports 30% fewer defect tickets after adoption across six customers; deployments increased 55% during the same period, selection criteria are undisclosed, and there is no control group.
- A randomized study of 120 developers on isolated coding tasks reports 26% faster completion but no statistically significant difference in task accuracy.
- An internal observational comparison across eight teams reports 18% fewer defects per release among assistant users; those users were more senior and increased automated test coverage by 20%.
- A survey of 800 developers finds 72% believe the assistants improve code quality; it measures perception, not defects.
- One internal postmortem attributes a production outage to an accepted AI-generated database migration.

Do not average incompatible numbers. Do not invent confidence intervals, causal effects, or missing study details.
